About Our Herbarium

Our herbarium houses a comprehensive collection of dried plant specimens, preserved according to international scientific standards. Each specimen includes accurate data such as species name, family, location, and collection date. The herbarium is designed to assist researchers, students, and nature enthusiasts in the exploration and understanding of plant biodiversity.
