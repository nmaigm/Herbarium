Zilla spinosa (L.) subsp. Spinosa.
Prantl in Engl.& Prantl, NatÜrl. Pflanzenfam.III(2):175(1891).

Synonyms:
Bunias spinosa L., Mant.96(1767).
Zilla microcarpa (DC.) Vis. in Spongia, Comment. Med. 2:209(1836).

Morphology:
Perennial spiny shrub, up to 150cm. Stem erect, longitudinally densely ribbed, solid and dichotomously branched, tapering to a sharp spine, older branches white and leafless. Internodes 2 – 4.2 cm. x o.2 - 0.4 cm. Leaves few, and glabrous. Lower leaves long ovate in outline with short petiole. Petiole up to 0.6 cm. long. Blade 1.5 - 3 cm. x 0.5 – 0.7 cm, simple, entire and acute apex. The upper leaves small and sessile. Blade 1 – 1.7 cm. x 0.5 cm. obovate in outline, entire with acute apex. Inflorescence raceme. Flowers 9 - 16 mm. long and pedicellate. Pedicels glabrous, short, 2 – 3 mm. long. Sepals glabrous, green, 5 - 7 mm. x1.5 - 2 mm. oblong-ovate in outline with narrow membranous margin. Petals violet 9 - 16 mm. x 2 – 3 mm. clawed, limb obovate with obtuse apex. Stamen 5 – 8 mm. long, with glabrous, filament and linear-ovate anthers. Ovary smooth with short style 6-8 mm. and long stigma. Silicula o.8 – 1.5 cm. x 0.5 – 1.1 cm., sub globose with six ribbed, glabrous, and indehiscent. Seed one per locule. Beak 2- 6 mm long, and seedless. Seeds oblong, 3– 3.5 mm. x 2 – 2.5 mm. dark brown with sub-terminal hilum, smooth surface and colour.
