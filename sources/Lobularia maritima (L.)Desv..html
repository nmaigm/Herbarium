Morphology:
Perennial herb, up to 30 cm. long, grayish white with bi-armed hairs. Stem erect, angular, solid and branched at the base. Internodes 2– 5 cm. x o.1 - 0.2 cm. Lower leaves simple and petiolate. Petiole up to 1.6 cm. long. Blade 3.2 – 5 cm. x 0.8 – 1 cm., long obovate with entire margin and acute apex. Upper leaves small with short petiole. Petiole up to 0.7 cm. long. Blade 1.2 – 2.2 cm. x 0.3 – 0.5 cm., elliptic with entire margin and acute apex. Inflorescence racemes. Flowers 2 - 3 mm. long and pedicellate. Pedicels hairy up to 10 mm. long. Sepals green, hairy, 1.8 -2 mm. x 0.5 – 1 mm., ovate in outline with ± wide membranous margin. Petals white 2 -3 mm. x 1 – 2 mm., clawed, limb sub orbicular with obtuse apices. Stamen 1 – 3 mm. long with glabrous, winged filament and ovate anthers. Ovary sparsely hairy with glabrous style, 2 – 3 mm. long and capitate stigma. Silicula 3 – 3.5 cm. x 0.1- 0.2 cm., compressed, sub-globose to sub-orbicular, dehiscent, with membranous septum and long beak 0.9 – 1mm. Seeds one per locule. Seeds 1.5 - 2 mm. x 1.3 -1.8 mm., ovate to sub-orbicular, with narrow membranous marginal wing, sub-terminal hilum, smooth surface and brownish colour.
