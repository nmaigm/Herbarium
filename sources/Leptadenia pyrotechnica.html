Morphology:
Leafless shrub 1.2-4 m; stems erect, much-branched, terete, green, spinescent; leaves (on juvenile branches) 0.5-1.5 x 0.2-0.35 cm, linear-lanceolate, sessile, acute, glabrous; flowers in axillary, few-flowered umbel-like cymes; peduncle 2-6 mm; pedicel 2-3 mm. pubescent, calyx 1-2 mm, pubescent; corolla 3-4 mm, broadly funnel-shaped, deeply divided, the lobes pubescent on both surfaces; follicle 8-12 x 0.5-0.7 cm, narrowly elliptic, terete, glabrous; seeds 6-8 mm; coma 2.5-4 cm.
