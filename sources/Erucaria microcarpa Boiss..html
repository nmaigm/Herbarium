Erucaria microcarpa Boiss
Diagn. Pl. Orient. 8:47 (1849).

Morphology:
Annual herb, up to 40 cm. long and glabrous. Stem erect -ascending, terete, solid and branched at the base. Internodes 1.5 – 2.5 cm. x o.2 - 0.3 cm. Leaves up to 6.5 cm. long. Lower leaves obovate in outline and petiolate. Petiole glabrous and up to 1.4 cm. long. Blade 3.2 -5 cm. x 3.5 – 4 cm, 3-4 pinnatisect with oblong lobes. The upper leaves ovate in outline and small. Petiole very short, o.5 cm. long. Blade 2.5 – 3.5 cm. x 2 - 2.5 cm. pinnatisect with oblong, entire lobes. Inflorescence raceme. Flowers 2 – 4 mm. long and pedicellate. Pedicels glabrous, 1.5 – 2.5 mm. long. Sepals glabrous, green to yellow, 1.9 - 2.5 mm. x 0.9- 1.2 mm., long-obovate in outline with narrow membranous margin. Petals white 2 - 4 mm. x 1 – 1.5 mm. clawed, limb obovate with obtuse apex. Stamen 1.9 – 3.5 mm. long, with glabrous, filament and oblong anthers. Ovary smooth with 1– 2mm. long style and capitate stigma. Siliqua 0.3 – o.5 cm. x 0.5 – 1mm. erect, glabrous, indehiscent with longitudinally-nerved and 2- segmented. The upper segment ovate, one seeded, shorter than lower segment. The lower segment oblong with 2-3 seeds. Beak 1-3 mm. long and seedless. Seeds slightly compressed, 0.5 – 1 mm. x 0.2 – 0.5 mm., broad ovate – semi globose with terminal hilum, glandular surface and brownish colour.
