Echinops macrochaetus Fresen.
Mus. Senckenb. 3: 69 (1845).

Morphology:
Erect perennial 30-80 cm; stems branched, often dark red, glandular-hairy above: leaves 6-18 x 3-6 cm, oblanceolate to elliptic, pinnatifid, glandular-puberulous on the upper surface, densely white-tomentose beneath, the base semi-amplexicaul; lobes broadly triangular, incised or dentate, the teeth and apex tapering to a spine; synflorescences 3-4 cm diam.: corollas blue, achenes densely setulose; pappus of staceous ciliate scales, connate at the base.
