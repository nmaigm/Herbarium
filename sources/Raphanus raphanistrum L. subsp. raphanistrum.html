Raphanus raphanistrum L.
Sp. Pl., ed. 1, 669 (1753) subsp. raphanistrum

Morphology:
Annual, glabrous to slightly hispid herb, up to 80 cm. long. Stem erect, terete, solid and basally branched. Internodes 3 – 5 cm. x o.2 - 0.3 cm. Leaves up to 11 cm. long. Lower leaves oblong-obovate in outline and petiolate. Petiole up to 2 cm. long. Blade 5.5 – 8.2 cm. x 5.5- 10 cm., lyrate-pinnatisect, with 4-5 ovate-triancular, dentate and acute lateral lobes. Terminal lobes obovate in outline with dentate and truncate apex. Upper leaves simple and petiolate. Petiole up to 1.3 cm. long. Blade 1.9 – 3.9 cm. x 0.8- 2 cm., oblong-ovate, dentate and acute apex. Inflorescence racemes. Flowers yellow 12 - 20 mm. long, and pedicellate. Pedicels with few hairs, 5 – 6 mm. Sepals yellowish-green, glabrous, 6 – 10 mm. x 1 – 2 mm., oblong-ovate in outline with narrow membranous margin. Petals yellow with violet nerves 13 - 20 mm. long x 1.5 – 2 mm. width, clawed, limb obovate with obtuse apices. Stamen 7 - 12 mm. long, with glabrous filament and oblong-ovate anthers. Ovary smooth with 3 – 4 mm. long, glabrous style and capitate stigma. Siliqua 3 – 5.5 cm. x 0.1 - 0.2 cm., compressed, indehiscent, slightly curved and segmented. Beak narrow, 2.5mm. long, seedless. Seeds in one row. Seeds broad ovoid - sub globose, 2.5 - 3 mm x 1.5 – 2 mm., with sub-terminal hilum, reticulate surface and brown colour.
