Ipomoea carnea Jacq.
Enum. Pl. Carib. 13 (1760).

Morphology:
Shrub 1.5-3.5 m; stems 1-2 cm diam., erect or spreading; leaf-blade 5-12 x 4-7 cm, ovate-lanceolate, the apex acute, the base shallowly cordate; flowers in dichasial cymes; sepals 4-5 x 3.5-4 mm, broadly ovate, coriaceous, puberulent, the margin narrowly scarious; corolla 5.5-7.5 cm, pink or deep pink to purplish; capsule 2-2.2 cm, subglobose to broadly ovoid; seeds clothed with brownish hairs.
