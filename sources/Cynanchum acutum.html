Cynanchum acutum L.
Sp. Pl., ed. 1, 212 (1753) subsp.acutum

Synonyms:
Cynanchum monspeliacum L., Sp. Pl., ed. 1, 212 (1753).

Morphology:
Glabrous or glabrescent perennial; stems to 2.5 m, twining; leaves 3-10 x 1.5-8 cm, cordate, the apex acute, the base broadly auriculate; petiole 1-3.5 cm; flowers in axillary cymes; peduncle 1-5 cm; pedicel 0.5-1 cm; calyx 1-2 mm, hairy, the teeth triangular; corolla 0.8-1.2 cm broad, pinkish-white, glabrous; lobes 4-5 mm, linear, acute; corona 5-lobed, the lobes caudate; follicles 6-12(-18) x 0.6-1 cm, the apex attenuate, glabrous.
