Volutaria tubuliflora (Murb.) Sennen.
Campagn. Bot. Maroc Orient. 1930-1935, in observ. (1936).

Synonyms:
Amberboa tubuliflora Murb., Act. Univ. Lund 33 (12): 105, t. 6, f. 17-18 (1897).

Morphology:
Like Volutaria lippii, but plant to 1.2 m; leaf-lobes sharply serrate; capitula solitary or in clusters of 2-4; phyllaries with brown margins and spiny acuminate apex; corolla purplish-pink; achenes 3.5-4 x 1 mm; pappus 2.5-3 mm.
