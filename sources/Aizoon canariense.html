Aizoon canariense L.
Sp. Pl., ed. 1, 488 (1753).

Synonyms:
Glinus chrystallinus Forssk., Fl. Aegypt.-Arab. 95 (1775).

Morphology:
Annual herb. papillose-pubescent or villose: stems 5-25 cm, procumbent or prostrate branched: leaves 1-3.5 x 0.5-1.5 cm, alternate, petiolate, spathulate to suborbicular flowers 4-6 mm, sessile; perianth 2-5 mm, broadly ovate to triangular, greenish outside yellowish inside; stamens 15-25; capsule 4-6 mm, flattened; seeds 1 mm, reniform, black.
