Gossypium barbadense L.
Sp. Pl., ed. 1, 693 (1753).

Synonyms:
Gossypium vitifolium Lam., Encycle. Meth. ed. 2, 135 (1786).
Gossypium Brasiliense Macf., Fl. Jam. ed. 1, 72 (1837).

Morphology:
Perennial shrub or annual subshrub. Leaves 2/3 cut into 3-5 lobes, lobes long, tapering, acuminate, slightly constricted at the base, the sinuses usually thrown up in folds. Staminal column long, anthers closely packed on short filaments of almost uniform size.
