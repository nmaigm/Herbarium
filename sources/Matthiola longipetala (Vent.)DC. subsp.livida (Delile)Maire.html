Matthiola longipetala (Vent) subsp. Livida (Delile).
Maire, FI. Afr. Nord 14: 23 (1977).

Synonyms:
Cheiranthus lividus Delile, Descr. Egypte, Hist.Nat. 67 (1814).
Matthiola Livida (Delile) DC., Syst. Nat. 2: 174 (1821).

Morphology:
Annual herb10 - 40 cm. long, pubescent, stipitate with glandular or eglandular and stellate hairs. Stem erect, compressed cylindrical, solid and branching from the base. Internodes 1.3 - 3.3 cm. x o.1 - 0.2 cm. Lower leaves simple and petiolate. Petiole up to 0.9 cm. long. Blade 2.1 – 2.8 cm. x 0.3 – 0.5 cm., oblong obovate-lanceolate with undulate margin and obtuse apex. Upper leaves small, simple with very short petiole. Petiole up to 0.5 cm. long. Blade 1 – 2.2 cm. x 0.1 – 0.3 cm., oblong obovate with entire margin and obtuse apex. Inflorescence racemes. Flowers 11 - 14 mm. long , sessile or short pedicellate. Pedicels hairy 1 - 2 mm. long. Sepals 6 – 8 mm. x 1.5 – 2 mm., green, linear-oblong in outline with narrow membranous margin and densed hairs. Petals violet, 11 - 14 mm. x 1.5 – 2 mm., clawed, limb linear-obovate, wavy margin and acute apex. Stamen 5 – 7 mm. long with glabrous, broad base filament and oblong anthers. Ovary hairy with 2 – 4 mm. long, hairy style and decurrent stigma. Siliqua 3 – 10 cm. x 0.1 – 0.3 cm., linear, terete, deflexed, contorted, hairy rarely glabrous. Horns present at pod apex, Stigmatic horns 2- 4 mm. long, straight or curved. Seeds in one row. Seeds small 1 – 1.3 mm. x 0.5-1 mm., oblong with sub-terminal hilum, smooth surface and yellowish brown colour.
