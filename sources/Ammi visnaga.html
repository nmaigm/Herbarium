Ammi visnaga (L.) Lam.
Fl.Fr. 3:462(1778).

Synonyms:
Daucus visnaga L., SP.PI., ed.1, 242(1753).

Morphology:
Both vegetative and floral characters resemble those in Ammi majus, differ only with regard to:
1- Blade of leaves, 3-pinnatisect with linear or filiform segment.
2- Bract pinnatisect into filiform segment, entire.
3- Petals with unequal bilobed.
4- Stylopodium depressed.
