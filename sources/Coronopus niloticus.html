Coronopus niloticus (Delile) Spreng.
Syst. Veg. 2: 853 (1825).

Synonyms:
Cochlearia nilotica Delile, Descr. Egypte, Hist. Nat. 101 (1814). Senebiera nilotica (Delile) DC., Syst. Nat. 2: 527 (1821).

Morphology:
Like C. didymus but leaves less finely divided, pinnatifid or serrate: stems almost glabrous; raceme longer; silicula rounded and apiculate at the tip.
