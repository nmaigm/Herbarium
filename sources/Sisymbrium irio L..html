Sisymbrium irio L.
Sp.Pl.,ed.1,659(1753).

Morphology:
Annual bright green herb, up to 50 cm. long, glabrous sometimes with short thin hairs. Stem erect, terete, solid and branched above. Internodes 1-2.5 cm. x o.1-0.3 cm. Leaves long and oblong- ovate in outline. Lower leaves petiolate and reach to 22 cm. long. Petiole glabrous and up to 6 cm. long. Blade 7.5 -16.5 cm. x 4 - 8 cm., lyrate-pinnatisect with large ovate terminal lobe and 3 - 4 pairs of oblong-linear toothed to entire, acute lateral lobes. The upper leaves are small with short petiole. Petiole up to 0.8 cm. long. Blade 2.5 – 5.5 cm. x 3 - 4.2 cm. with hastate, dentate terminal lobe and 2-3 pairs of linear, entire and acute lateral lobes. Inflorescences are racemes. Flowers bisexual, actinomorphic, hypogynous, yellow 3 - 5 mm. long and pedicellate. Pedicels 3.5 - 7 mm. long, thin with few hairs. Calyx, four sepals. Sepals yellowish green, glabrous, oblong 2 -3 mm. x 1 mm. Corolla, four petals. Petals yellow, 2.5 - 3 mm. x 1 mm., clawed, limb oblong-obovate with obtuse emarginated apex. Androecium, six stamens, tetra-dynamous. Stamen 2 – 3.2 mm. long with glabrous filament and triangular -ovate anthers. Gynoecium, two carpels, parietal placentation. Ovary smooth with glabrous style, 0.6-1.2 mm. long and bi-lobed stigma. Siliqua 1.5 – 4cm. x o.1 cm., linear, erect or slightly curved, glabrous, dehiscent and torulose with distinct mid-vein. Valves membranous, 20- 40 seeds in each locule and white membranous septum, beak 1mm. long. Seeds minute, 1 – 1.5 mm. long x 0.3 – 0.5 mm. width, oblong-ellipsoidal, sub-terminal hilum, smooth surface and yellowish brown colour.
