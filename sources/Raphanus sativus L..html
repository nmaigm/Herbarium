Raphanus sativus L.
Sp. Pl., 699. (1753).

Morphology:
Annual or biennial glabrous to slightly hispid herb, up to 120 cm. long. Stem erect, terete, solid and branched from the base. Internodes 1.8 – 3.3 cm. x o.2 - 0.3 cm. Leaves obovate in outline, up to 23 cm. long. Lower leaves long and petiolate. Petiole up to 10.4 cm. long. Blade 8.7 – 12.5 cm. x 3.5- 4.5 cm., lyrate-pinnatifid with dentae and acute apex. Upper leaves simple and petiolate. Petiole up to 2.5 cm. long. Blade 3.3 – 6 cm. x 2- 2.5 cm., oblong ovate with dentate-sinuate margin and acute apex. Inflorescence corymb. Flowers 12 - 18 mm. long, and pedicellate. Pedicels glabrous, 7 – 10 mm. long reach to 15 mm. long in fruit. Sepals yellowish-green, glabrous, oblong - elliptic, 8 – 10 mm. x 1.5 – 2 mm. with narrow membranous margin. Petals violet with dark veins, 11 - 18 mm. long x 1.5 – 2 mm. width, clawed, limb obovate with obtuse apices. Stamen 8 - 10 mm. long, with glabrous filament and oblong-ovate anthers. Ovary smooth with 6 – 8 mm. long, glabrous style and bi-lobed stigma. Siliqua 1.7 – 3.6 cm. x 0.3 - 0.6 cm., cylindrical, indehiscent, slightly curved and segmented. Beak long narrow, 1.5mm. seedless. Seeds in one row. Seeds subglobose-broad ovate, 3- 4 mm x 2 – 2.8 mm., dark brown with terminal hilum and reticulate surface.
