Capsella bursa-pastoris (L.)
Medik., Pfl.-Gatt. 85 (1792).

Synonyms:
Thlaspi bursa-pastoris L., Sp. Pl., ed. 1, 647 (1753).

Morphology:
Annual, herb, glabrous to sparingly hairy, up to 50 cm. long. Stem erect, terete, solid and branched from the base. Internodes 1.9 – 3 cm. x o.2 - 0.3 cm. Lower leaves forming a rosette, oblong-obovate to oblong-lanceolate in outline and petiolate. Petiole up to 3.5 cm. long. Blade 7 – 14.5 cm. x 5.5- 10 cm., lyrate-pinnatifid with coarsely dentate margin and acute apex. Upper leaves sessile, simple, oblong-obovate, sagitate at the base, serrate-dentate or entire and acute apex, 4.3 – 8.5 cm. x. Inflorescence racemes. Flowers 1 – 2 mm. long, and pedicellate. Pedicels glabrous, 6 – 11 mm long. Sepals yellowish-green, hairy, 1 – 2 mm. x 1 mm., ovate in outline with narrow membranous margin. Petals white, 1 – 2 mm. x 0.5 mm., clawed, limb broad obovate with obtuse apex. Stamen 1 - 2 mm. long, with glabrous filament and ovate anthers. Ovary smooth with 1 – 1.5 mm. long, glabrous style and capitate stigma. Silicula 5 – 10 mm. x 4 – 6 mm., cordite- obtrianqular, laterally compressed, dehiscent, glabrous, lateral margin usually straight. Valve keeled, reticulate-veined. Seeds about 12 per locule. Beak 0.5 mm. long, seedless. Seeds 1 – 1.5 mm x o.2 - 0.5 mm., oblong-ellipsoid with terminal hilum, smooth surface and brown colour.
