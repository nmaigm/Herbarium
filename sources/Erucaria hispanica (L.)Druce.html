Erucaria hispanica (L.).
Druce, Bot. Exch. Club Soc. Brit. Isles 3:418 (1914).

Synonyms:
Sinapis hispanica L., Sp. Pl., ed. 1, 669 (1753).
Bunias myagroides L., Mant. 96 (1767).
Erucaria aleppica Gaertn., Fruct. 2:298 (1791).
Erucaria latifolia DC., Syst. Nat. 2:675 (1821).
Erucaria myagroides (L.) Halascy, Consp. Fl. Gr. 1: 123 (1900).

Morphology:
Annual herb, glabrous, up to 60 cm. long. Stem erect- ascending, terete, solid and basely branched. Internodes 3– 5.5 cm. x o.2 - 0.4 cm. Leaves up to 19 cm. long. Lower leaves ovate in outline, petiolate. Petiole glabrous, long; 6.5 cm. Blade 5.5 -12.4 cm. x 1.5 – 2.5 cm, pinnatisect with narrow oblong, dentate or entire lobes. The upper leaves small with very short petiole. Petiole up to 0.6 cm. long. Blade 3 – 4 cm. x 3-3.5 cm. ovate in outline, pinnatisect with narrow-oblong, entire and acute lobes. Inflorescence raceme. Flowers 7 - 11 mm. long and pedicellate. Pedicels glabrous, 2 mm. long. Sepals glabrous, yellow to violet, 3 - 6mm. x1- 1.5 mm., long ovate in outline with narrow membranous margin. Petals violet 7 - 10 mm. x 1 – 2.5 mm. clawed, limb obovate with obtuse apex. Stamen 4 – 7 mm. long, with glabrous, filament and ovate anthers. Ovary smooth, long style 3– 4mm. and capitate stigma. Siliqua 1.2 – 2 cm. x 1 – 1.5 mm., straight or slightly curved, glabrous, indehiscent with longitudinally-nerved and 2- segmented. The upper segment ovate-oblong with 2-seeded, shorter than lower segment. The lower segment oblong, 2-5 seeds. Beak 3-4 mm. long and seedless. Seeds 1 – 1.5 mm. x 0.5 -1 mm., slightly compressed, oblong-ovate with sub-terminal hilum, smooth surface and pale brown colour.
